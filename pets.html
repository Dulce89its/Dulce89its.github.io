Pets

Sheltie I

Murphy

My wife adopted our dog Murphy in October of 2012. She saw a picture of him online and it was love at first sight.

She met Murphy at a local shelter (SICSA) and brought him home that same day.

On the drive home, Murphy put his head in her lap. It was love at first sight for him too.

I first met Murphy in October of 2017 and we have been together ever since.

Great things about Murphy:

  • He loves to snuggle
  • He loves food (maybe too much)
  • He is a great older brother to Isabelle
  • He is incredibly sweet

Sheltie II

Isabelle

My wife and I adopted Isabelle shortly after we became engaged and I moved back to Dayton (my home town) from Cincinnati.

We were interested in finding a playmate for Murphy, and he was so great we decided we needed another Sheltie.

We brought Izzy home in March of 2019, and she has been a source of enrichment (and trouble) ever since.

Great things about Izzy:

  • She's adorable
  • She is extremely fast and quick
  • She's a great motivater to get outside and go on walks
  • She's almost as good a snuggler as Murphy
