Honeymoon

Honeymoon Part I

Chicago

My wife and I had a two-part Honeymoon: we spent about a week in Chicago and a couple of nights in Nashville.

While in Chicago, one of our favorite things that we did was visiting the Garfield Park Conservatory.

We also went on a boat tour of the city that gave us a great opportunity to see some fantastic Chicago architecture.

Cocktail with dry ice at "Two Dots and a Dash".

Honeymoon Part II

Nashville

The second part of my honeymoon was Nashville.

My cousin, Jeff Fenter, was out of town and let us stay at his downtown apartment for a weekend.

Nashville was a fun place with great music and fantastic food.

I hope to go back sometime soon.
