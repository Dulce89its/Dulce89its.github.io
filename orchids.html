Plants

Orchids

I first became interested in orchids circa 2017, and my first few orchids were common varieties that I purchased from Kroger or Meijer.

Since then, I have acquired dozens more and I currently have more than 80 orchids in my collection.

My wife and I also joined our local orchid society, Miami Valley Orchid Society.

Fun facts about orchids:

  • There are tens of thousands of orchid species
  • Vanilla comes from an orchid
  • There are more than 100,000 orchid varieties

Houseplants

Non-Orchids

My love of orchids has spilled over into a more general love of exotic plants.

I now have dozens of non-orchid houseplants, and I'm finding new, interesting plants to add to my collection all the time.
